About CESS

Who We Are

The Civil Engineering Students Society — popularly known as CESS — at NIT, Arunachal Pradesh is a non-profit organization within the Department of Civil Engineering. Our members include Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate students, faculty members, and alumni.

We work together to facilitate the overall development of students pursuing Civil Engineering at NITAP. CESS provides a platform for students to showcase and sharpen their talents through a variety of events and activities planned throughout the year.

Purpose

Mission & Objectives

To explore the hidden talent in students & keep them abreast with the latest trends in Civil Engineering — that is the humble motive behind "CESS: Civil Engineering Student's Society".

01

Professional Readiness

Expose potential civil engineers to the challenges of the profession and proactively enlighten them with current trends in Civil Engineering.

02

Continuous Learning

Involve students in Guest Lectures, Seminars, and Industrial Visits throughout the academic calendar to keep them updated with the latest advancements.

03

Innovation Culture

Encourage every student to put forward innovative ideas through meetings, discussions, debates, and seminars in an open and collaborative environment.
